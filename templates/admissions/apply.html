Admission Process

Simple steps to join our school community

1
Submit Application

Complete our online application form or visit the school to collect a physical form.

  • Online application available
  • Physical forms at school
  • No application fee
2
Document Submission

Provide all required documents including academic records and certificates.

  • KCPE certificate
  • Birth certificate
  • Academic records
3
Interview & Assessment

Meet with our admissions team for a brief interview and academic assessment.

  • Student interview
  • Parent meeting
  • Academic assessment
4
Enrollment

Complete enrollment by paying fees and collecting your admission letter and school materials.

  • Fee payment
  • Admission letter
  • School materials
