Admission Requirements

Please ensure you have all the required documents for a smooth application process

Academic Documents

KCPE certificate or equivalent, previous school reports, and academic transcripts

Identification

Birth certificate, national ID card (for students 18+), and passport-size photographs

Medical Records

Medical certificate, immunization records, and any special medical requirements

Financial Documents

Proof of financial capability and completed fee payment forms
