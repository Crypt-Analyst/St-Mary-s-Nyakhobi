Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original and copy)
  • Recent academic reports
  • 2 recent passport-size photos
  • Transfer letter (if applicable)
  • Parent/Guardian ID copy

Admission Criteria

  • Grade 10: Completion of Junior Secondary (Grade 9)
  • Grade 11: Successful completion of Grade 10
  • Grade 12: Successful completion of Grade 11
  • Transfer Students: Academic transcripts required
  • Good conduct record from previous school
