St. Mary's Nyakhobi Senior School (formerly Nyakhobi Secondary School) is located in Nyakhobi Sub-location, Namboboto Location, Samia Sub-County, Busia County, Kenya. The school is sponsored by ACK Church Nambale Diocese.

School History

The school was started in 1983 with 6 students through the efforts of Nyakhobi Community operating at home and in towns, particularly in Nairobi. They came together as Nyakhobi Building Committee and raised funds on monthly basis to build the first tuition block (4 classrooms). It was registered in 1987 by the Ministry of Education.

The school grew up from Nyakhobi Primary School where it shared the land until September 6, 2017 when it got the title deed (2.221A). Starting from 2026, the school will be known as St. Mary's Nyakhobi Senior School.
