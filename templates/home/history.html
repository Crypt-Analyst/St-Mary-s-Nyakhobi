Our History

Over 40 Years of Educational Excellence (1983-2025)

A Legacy of Excellence

Since our founding, St. Mary's Nyakhobi Senior School has been committed to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders. Our journey spans over four decades of unwavering dedication to academic excellence and character development.

Our Journey Through Time

1980

Foundation

St. Mary's Nyakhobi Senior School was established with a vision to provide quality secondary education to the children of Nyakhobi and surrounding areas. Started with just 50 students and 8 teachers in temporary structures.

1985

First Permanent Buildings

Construction of the first permanent classroom blocks and administration offices was completed. The school population grew to 200 students with expanded teaching staff and improved facilities.

1990

Academic Recognition

The school achieved its first major academic milestone with excellent KCSE results, establishing itself as a leading institution in the region. Introduction of science laboratories and library facilities.

2000

Modernization Era

Introduction of computer studies and modern teaching methodologies. Construction of new dormitories and dining hall to accommodate boarding students. Student population reached 500.

2010

Infrastructure Expansion

Major infrastructure development including new classroom blocks, upgraded laboratories, and sports facilities. Implementation of ICT integration in learning and teaching processes.

2020

Digital Transformation

Adaptation to digital learning during the pandemic era. Introduction of e-learning platforms and enhanced ICT infrastructure to support modern education delivery methods.

2025

Continuous Growth

Ongoing construction of new laboratory building and additional classrooms with support from Funyula NG-CDF. Commitment to maintaining high academic standards while expanding opportunities for all students.
