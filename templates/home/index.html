CBC Senior Secondary Education

St. Mary's Nyakhobi Senior School is a progressive institution dedicated to implementing Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) for Senior Secondary learners. We offer comprehensive education for Grade 10, Grade 11, and Grade 12 students, preparing them for the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KSCE) examination.

Our school provides a nurturing environment where students develop critical thinking, practical skills, and strong character values. We offer over 40 CBC subject combinations across STEM, Social Sciences, Arts, and Sports pathways, ensuring every student finds their path to success.

Located in Funyula, Busia County, we serve the community with quality education, modern facilities, and dedicated teachers committed to academic excellence and holistic development.

Foundation year for Senior Secondary. Students explore diverse CBC subjects, develop core competencies, and begin specializing in their chosen pathway (STEM, Social Sciences, Arts, or Sports).

Grade 11

Skills development year. Students deepen their understanding of chosen subjects, engage in practical projects, and prepare for advanced learning. Focus on competency mastery and real-world application.

Specialization and KSCE preparation year. Students complete their Senior Secondary education, sit for national examinations, and prepare for tertiary education or careers. Intensive exam preparation and guidance.
