Mission & Vision

Our Purpose & Future Direction

Our Motto

"Sacrifice for Success"

This motto embodies our belief that excellence requires dedication, hard work, and commitment from both students and staff.

Our Mission

To consistently provide quality CBC senior secondary education that prepares students for KSCE excellence and future success.

Our Vision

To be an institution of excellence in pursuit of quality and holistic education, nurturing well-rounded individuals.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academic achievement, character development, and all school activities.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all interactions within our school community.

Respect

We treat everyone with dignity and value diversity, fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment.

Innovation

We embrace creative approaches to learning and adapt to meet the demands of modern education.

Community

We build strong partnerships with parents, staff, and the wider community to support student success.

Faith

As an ACK-sponsored school, we integrate Christian values and principles into our educational approach.

Our Educational Philosophy

At St. Mary's Nyakhobi Senior School, we believe in developing the whole student - academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

Student-Centered Learning

We place students at the center of the learning experience, recognizing their unique abilities and learning styles through the CBC framework.

Competency-Based Curriculum

Our CBC programs focus on developing practical skills and competencies that prepare students for real-world challenges beyond the classroom.

Holistic Development

We nurture academic excellence while also developing character, leadership, critical thinking, and social responsibility.

Continuous Improvement

We constantly evaluate and improve our programs, facilities, and teaching methods to provide the best possible education.
