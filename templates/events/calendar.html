Outstanding KCSE Results 2024

Published: March 15, 2025

St. Mary's Nyakhobi Senior School celebrates exceptional performance in the 2024 KCSE examinations with a mean score of 8.9 points.

Mean Score: 8.9 97% University Qualification 15 A- Grades
  • ✓ 45 students qualified for direct university admission
  • ✓ Top performer: Sarah Wanjiku with A- (81 points)
  • ✓ Best subjects: Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology
  • ✓ Improvement: 12% increase from previous year

Regional Science Competition Victory

Published: September 8, 2025

Our students won first place in the Busia County Science and Mathematics Competition, demonstrating exceptional problem-solving skills.

1st Place Physics Category KES 50,000 Prize
  • ✓ Winners: Form 3 Physics Team
  • ✓ Project: Renewable Energy Solutions
  • ✓ Competition: 25 schools participated
  • ✓ Next step: National level competition

Athletics Championship Success

Published: August 22, 2025

St. Mary's athletics team dominated the Western Kenya Secondary Schools Championships with multiple gold medals.

5 Gold Medals 3 Silver Medals Overall 2nd Place
  • ✓ Star athlete: John Odhiambo (1500m, 5000m gold)
  • ✓ Girls relay: 4x100m gold medal
  • ✓ Records broken: 2 new school records
  • ✓ Nationals: 8 students qualified

New Laboratory Equipment

Published: July 10, 2025

The school has acquired state-of-the-art laboratory equipment worth KES 2.5 million to enhance science education.

KES 2.5M Investment 3 Labs Upgraded Modern Equipment
  • ✓ Chemistry lab: New microscopes and analyzers
  • ✓ Physics lab: Digital measuring instruments
  • ✓ Biology lab: Advanced specimen collection
  • ✓ Benefit: Enhanced practical learning
